This study was conducted throughout the start of 2026. 
Out of interrest i recorded 100 individuals phone usage on the s train. 
The recordings were primarialy made on line E and A from Lyngby to Fisketorvet station.

Three different categories where chosen to be recorded. 
These include: **[looking at phone], [listening on headphones/earbuds], [talking on phone/headphones]** and **[not interracting with electronnics]**

Before reading the rest of this study you might try guessing what the results will be!
